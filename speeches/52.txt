The Ideal World Desired by All People  
I would like to speak under the title "The Ideal World Desired by All People." This is a very grand title.  
This world is a wrong world that started from a wrong motive.

Therefore, the question of when and in what condition the United States will move forward is the biggest and most important issue that attracts people's attention. Many people long for an ideal world overflowing with freedom and peace, and we can easily understand such a longing. Given the current trends in the world, we cannot achieve the ideal world that all people desire. Therefore, our current goals are very unclear and not precise.  
Can we find a country that can take responsibility for building such an ideal world? I believe that the people living in the United States today cannot lead this world to such an ideal world. I think the people living in the communist world cannot do so even more. Although the Soviet Union is a growing country, they cannot achieve such a unified world. For this reason, God has worked more through smaller countries.  
In the United States, we cannot find an ideal that can make people love the world more than their own country. Therefore, the United States cannot be a leading country in building such an ideal world. Likewise, the communist country of the Soviet Union also cannot be a leading country in building such an ideal world. Until the American people can sacrifice themselves for the benefit of all humanity and the world, there cannot be a world of prosperity, happiness, or peace. Likewise, the Soviet Union... If they cannot sacrifice their own country for the benefit of the world, God's providence cannot be fulfilled, and a world of peace or an ideal world cannot be completed.  
If God truly exists, would He love the world more than the United States, or love the United States more than the world? If a country comes forward and sacrifices its own country, loving the world more than its own, that country will become the nation that rules the entire world.  
Everything that develops is developing from its original motive, but the current world of results is not the world that all humanity desires. Therefore, we can see that the motives of the current world are not right. They are wrong. In other words, this world started from a wrong motive.

You are made up of both your past self and your new self. Among them, your new self is the hope. There is also an old family and a new family of hope. Your children must also renew themselves to become true sons and daughters. If you do not do this, on personal, family, tribal, national, and global levels...
there is no one who would want to meet. When you meet someone, it is because you expect a positive outcome from that interaction. 

In the same way, if God exists as an absolute being, then it is essential for Him to have a counterpart. If God were to exist in isolation, it would not be a happy existence. If the President of the United States were to be alone, smiling and laughing, even if He were God, it would not be a normal situation. However, if that God were to find joy in the presence of even a small counterpart, that would be considered normal. No matter how small the counterpart may be, if someone were to call that laughing God insane, it would be the accuser who is truly insane. Therefore, I want to emphasize how precious the existence of a counterpart is.

If God loves a counterpart, what kind of existence do you think He would prefer in this world? Would it be an insensate mineral? No. It would not even be a plant. Among the creations, animals might be of some interest. When we consider what the central being that could attract God's attention among His creations is, it would undoubtedly point to humans, even if it were a kindergarten student. We cannot deny that humans are the greatest masterpieces among all creations. Thus, if God seeks a counterpart, it leads to the conclusion that there is no other being to seek other than humans.

In today’s complex circumstances, when we think about the future fate of America or the world, the question of whether it is better for God to exist or not is a serious matter to contemplate. It would be ideal if an absolute being could judge good as good and evil as evil, but if such a being does not exist, the future of the world becomes a problem. Therefore, our wish must be that God exists rather than not. The question then becomes whether God exists or not.

The God of Reason and Substance
We know that the universe is a realm of existence. This is acknowledged by everyone. We understand that to exist, there must be power. The question arises as to whether power comes before existence or existence comes before power. If there is no power, there is no existence. Power does not manifest without action. Before power, the phenomenon of action is absolutely necessary. For action to occur, the conditions of subject and object must be present as prerequisites. It is undeniable that the action occurs in the exchange between the subject and object, and through this action, power is sustained.

What must exist before power is the subject and the object. Because the subject and object are in different positions, for them to act, they must correspond to each other in a relative context; otherwise, no action will take place. Therefore, when the subject and object act, we must look for the point where the purpose of the subject and the purpose of the object can align. If this alignment does not occur, no action will take place.

For us humans, when we go out for any reason, we do not engage in activities that would lead to our loss. In Times Square in New York, many people are coming and going, but no one leaves their home saying, "I am going out today to incur a loss." When people leave, they do so with the expectation of gaining something beneficial, something that will add to their lives. 

Even when friends meet or when they like each other, they do not seek to be in a situation where they could potentially lose or be worse off than they are now.
It is something that absolutely does not combine. However, when a person named A and a person named B look at each other's purposes, which can be better than now, there is an action taking place, and the act of combining into one occurs. From this perspective, we can conclude that the force acting has a dual purpose, meaning that without the mutual purposes of the subject and the object being added together, they cannot combine into one nor act.

When we look at atoms, in an atom, protons are at the center and electrons are revolving around them. This is also because they can combine two purposes, being in a position of giving and receiving, which forms a single atomic shape. Thus, they are combined around a purpose that encompasses both the purpose of the electron and the purpose of the proton. In a broader sense, God also exists as a being. If God exists, He exists as a real entity. Therefore, God must also be in a subject-object relationship, and without being in a position of dual purpose that can completely give and receive, He cannot exist eternally. This means that God ultimately combines subjectivity and objectivity and exists as one God.

The existence of God seen through human conscience
When we look at Genesis 1:27 in the Bible, it says, "God created man in His own image; in the image of God He created him; male and female He created them." If we pursue this inductively, we conclude that God is a being who combines one male and one female. Such a God cannot be alone, so He had to create this world out of necessity for an object. Thus, what was created is one man and one woman. If we look at humans, we see that a person has both a mind and a body. This mind and body interact with each other. If this interaction goes wrong, one experiences pain. One side tilts. However, when they combine and rise together, one becomes joyful. 

If there is a completely unified man, he would be a perfect man. If there is an absolute being, what kind of man would He demand? He would demand such a man. The same goes for women. If there is a woman whose mind and body are completely united, God cannot help but want to meet such a woman. Even considering the dignity of the absolute being, one must meet the best man and the best woman. Heaven desires this, and it requires a person who can stand confidently before Heaven, and we also demand this.

When we look at ourselves as humans, we cannot deny that we are not causal beings. Humans are resultative beings. Can you freely choose to exist or not? No. Since we cannot be the cause, we cannot deny that we are in a resultative position. That resultative existence can stand in a position of absolute relativity if the cause is absolute.

The existence of God seen through the mystery of the human body
Let’s briefly talk about whether God exists or not. If we trace back to the origin of humanity, we will find the first ancestor of humanity. This ancestor was born on the earth. When this first human was born on the earth, did he come out knowing that there was a sun that needed to be seen with his eyes, or did he come out not knowing that? When he came out to this earth and his eyes were able to see the sun, even if he did not know it, there was already sunlight in the celestial body, and he knew that he could see it, which is why his eyes were formed that way.

If you look at your eyes, they blink. This is because they know that they need to moisten themselves as the heat from the earth causes the moisture in the eyes to evaporate. Also, the eyebrows are there to prevent dust because they know that dust needs to be blocked. This shows that humans are born based on biological and astronomical knowledge.

Look at your eyes; they already knew they would sweat. They knew that if foreign substances entered these precious eyes, it would be a big problem, so they created barriers. Also, they knew that humans would stand and walk. Look at your ears; they were already formed to hear sound waves from the opposite direction. Look at your nose; it is shaped this way so that sweat does not enter the mouth, creating barriers around the mouth. Look at the nose hairs inside the nose; everything was created with knowledge. Can they just appear randomly? When we look at the entire human body, it resembles a palace of mystery, and this cannot just be as it is.

When humans are born, they themselves may not know it, but the question is whether there was someone who knew it or not. One cannot say that they were born without knowing how they would be born. The one who knew that in advance is God.

The inherent value of humans in relation to God
If God created humans, what position did He place us in, and what kind of relationship did He intend to establish? If God created humans, there must have been a purpose behind the creation. Moreover, if humans were created, they too must have a purpose. The purpose of the created being and the purpose of the Creator cannot be separate. There must be some point of agreement. That position is something that both God desires and we humans desire.

Among the people here, is there anyone who wants to lower themselves below a world leader? Do you want to rise or lower? You want to rise. You want to rise to the highest. If there is someone at the top of the world, it is human desire to want to establish a relationship with that person. Even if one has become one with that high person, if there is someone higher, one wants to become one with that person too. This is a mindset shared by both men and women. No matter how unattractive a person may be, they all have such thoughts. If there is an absolute being who created the heavens and the earth, would you want to establish a relationship with that God or not?

Even if one claims to have conquered God, if they cannot conquer the one and only love within God, they cannot feel at ease. If one conquers God and also conquers that one and only love within Him, then everything is okay. On the day one conquers only that love, what belongs to Him becomes theirs.

The action that drives us to that point is something we cannot deny because there is an inherent value position of humans that allows for such action.

If God is a God of love and wants to love us humans, in what position would He want to love? And in what position would we humans want to receive God's love? When we pursue this, that position can only be the parent-child relationship, that is, the relationship between a father and a son. Therefore, calling God "Father" in Christianity today is a valid expression for humans.

If there is a father, then the one and only father cannot be placed far away. One does not want to place Him far away. One wants to keep Him close, in a position where only they can meet and only they can cherish. This is why humans desire God to be invisible. One does not want to place God somewhere else. The desire of humans is to keep Him in their hearts where only they can know Him. Therefore, God, the Creator, wants to place Himself and us, His creatures, in the same position. He allows us to be loved in the position of His sons and to be equal with God...

The third point is that, upon careful observation, God is a being with the power to create the heavens and the earth, and humans want to possess that creative power as well. After God created humans, He found them so precious and lovable that He wanted to let humans feel that joy as well.

To receive God's creative power, God exists in the form of a perfect man and woman within one. Therefore, this perfect man and woman who were born separately must become one to be God's counterpart. By becoming God's counterpart, they can fulfill the creative role.

Thus, through the love of a couple, by becoming one, children can achieve the creative ability in a position similar to God. God wanted us to feel how joyful it was for Him to have children, which is why He gave us the heart to love children. From this perspective, it can be seen that God has poured out the best things before us humans. He created us to live in eternal happiness and satisfaction, united with the eternal love of God, in a peaceful Eden. Therefore, in front of the absolute God, we humans must live as happy beings who are eternally in a position of being loved by God's love, a state that cannot be separated.

The mission of religion
It is certain that the beings created by this absolute God must have a single purpose. However, do you think there are humans standing in a position of absolute relativity centered around God's love? No. Since the absolute God created them, humans should be aiming for a single purpose, yet when we look at ourselves, we know that we are on another path of contradictory purposes. Some people see this as a subject-object relationship in humans, but it is not.

If the action of conscience is an inner plus, then this (selfishness) is another plus. Thus, a backlash occurs. Standing in such a position is what our lives on this earth are.

We must recognize that we have lost the noble ideals of love and absolute values that God had given us, and today we are miserable humans groaning in a contradictory dual reality. In other words, we have become broken people. However, because the absolute God must relate to humans, He cannot leave us as we are, so He must carry out a movement to regenerate us through the historical process. That means He must create a repair factory. What is that? It is religion.

There are countless religions in the world. They are established based on different cultures or ethnic backgrounds. The purpose of religion is to save the world. The purpose that God desires is also to save the world. A religion that does not aim to save the world will not last long.

Religious leaders are not nationalists; they are globalists and have presented a movement centered on heaven. We know that there are four great saints, namely Jesus Christ, Buddha, Confucius, and Muhammad. Some people include Socrates. Socrates is a philosopher but cannot be considered a saint. Knowledge alone cannot save lives. Only heaven governs life. Therefore, all four great saints are the founders of major world religions. No one has served heaven without exception. 

You should respect the saints, but you must not disregard heaven.

The central world religion is Christianity
Now, regarding the mission of religion, if God is the absolute being, what kind of religion would He like the most among these four great religions? This is the question we must consider.

Looking at Confucianism, it does not teach anything definitive about God. It only teaches that if one does good, heaven will bless them, and if one does evil, they will be punished. Buddhism recognizes a legal deity but does not know a personal God. Looking at Islam, it is an intermediate religion that inherits Christianity. It is a forceful religion that demands adherence to the Quran or the sword. Such things do not please heaven.

However, only Jesus Christ asserted something different. He appeared and claimed, "I am the only begotten Son of God." No one else in the history of humanity has claimed to be the only begotten Son of God except Jesus. He also said, "Whoever has seen me has seen the Father." He stated, "I am in you, and you are in me, and the Father is in me, and I am in the Father." Furthermore, He said, "I am the bridegroom, and you are the bride."

When we see this, we realize that He intended to achieve this centered on God. In other words, He wanted to become one with God, to become sons and daughters, to form one body, and to establish one bride and groom, thereby forming one family. The position given to us humans as the highest value, equal to the position of the Son, and the joy of creation after creation, is what Jesus taught us.

The limitations of Christianity
Jesus was the Son of God and became one with God the Father, but He could not establish the position of a bride and groom with whom He could relate. If the nation of Israel and Judaism had allowed Jesus to welcome a bride in that unified position, Jesus would have had the foundation to directly relate to God and exercise the creative power, but that could not be achieved.

If the sons and daughters centered on God had come forth and formed families, it would have led to new tribes, nations, and a new world. This earth would have become the kingdom of heaven. The people of Israel did not believe, and the Jews did not believe, which led to Jesus' death. If they had believed, Jesus would not have died. If at that time, the people of Israel and Judaism had believed in Jesus and welcomed the bridegroom that God had desired, a new beginning would have been seen. The suffering of the Jewish people during World War II, where six million Jews were slaughtered by Hitler, would not have occurred. It happened because they struck down the one who was to be God's object and whom God could love. There is no greater sin than that.

Now, it seems that many people who believe in Jesus have come here. The current Christianity cannot suffice. If it continues like this, it may face the same fate as the people of Israel. Today, Christians scattered all over the world must unite centered on God, for the Lord who is to return comes with this mission, so that there will be no more expulsion.
Without practicing the exercise of brushing our teeth, we cannot help but see a bleak outlook for the future of this world.  
The coming of the Lord to hold a wedding feast as a bride is the feast of the Lamb mentioned in the Book of Revelation. Because of the fall, we have fallen false parents. The good true parents that God envisioned as a model have not appeared. This means that what was planned must be reassembled at the end. Unless that true parent comes out to give birth again and to graft us again, no one in the world can be saved. You must clearly understand that.  
The Unification Church, which has emerged to build a new world of love  
What mission does the Lord who is coming have? Because we have lost the true parents who should see completion from the position of good parents without falling, the one who comes is the returning Lord, who will reopen the way for humanity to be loved again from the family of true parents who are loved by God.  
It would be happiness for the sons and daughters who receive God's love to live on this earth and go to heaven as a family, wouldn't it? Is it happiness, as believed in today’s Christianity, for the mother to go to heaven and the father to go to hell, separated and going their own ways? It means that the whole must enter from parents to children. Thus, that family must enter, the tribe must enter, the nation must enter, and the world must all enter.  
A global family centered on these new parents will emerge, and from there, a new culture and tradition centered on a new ideal of love, which has never existed in human history, will manifest on this earth. When this happens, this complex and sinful world will become a new paradise on earth.  
To achieve this, you must know in detail how to proceed from here. Therefore, we see it as our mission in the Unification Church to teach such content. I know that there were many leaps and unreasonable points in trying to condense such vast content into a short time, but I sincerely thank you for your understanding and for listening.
